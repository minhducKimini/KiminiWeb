Expanding access to blood testing

Kimini enables reliable blood sampling for patients with difficult venous access while supporting decentralized diagnostic workflows.

Difficult venous access

Reliable blood collection for patients with small, fragile, or hardened veins, including oncology, dialysis, and frequently tested patients.

Remote blood diagnostics

Enable blood sampling in home care and community settings without requiring trained phlebotomists.

Improved patient satisfaction

Reduce discomfort associated with conventional venipuncture, improving patient satisfaction during diagnostic testing.
