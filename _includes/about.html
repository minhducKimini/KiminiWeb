Journey

From concept exploration to clinical-ready blood collection technology.

  • 2024

    Concept Development

    Initial concept of a painless capillary blood collection device, with early exploration of microneedle puncture and vacuum-assisted extraction.

  • 2025

    Engineering Validation

    Optimization of microneedle geometry, actuation consistency, and vacuum-assisted extraction to improve yield and usability.

  • January 2026

    Company Formation

    KiMiNi Medical was incorporated in Singapore to advance productization, partnerships, and clinical preparation.

  • 2027

    Clinical Validation and commercialization

    Planned clinical studies to validate sample quality, blood yield, and compatibility with standard diagnostic workflows.
